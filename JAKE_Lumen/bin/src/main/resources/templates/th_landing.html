JAKE

Understand the business's needs and integrate technology to develop a powerful information system that will lead to success.

Through an application, LiteUpAfrica wants to have their own carbon credit management system and streamline the process. The proposed application is a web application as it will be easier to access and maintain. The goal is to have a one-stop shop for carbon credit management using the website. In addition of having their own management system, LiteUpAfrica would also want to offer the system to other organizations that are interested in selling their carbon offsets.
